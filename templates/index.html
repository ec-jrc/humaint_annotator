Welcome to the HUMAINT image annotator!


With this tool you will be able to annotate images coming from autonomous vehicles datasets. The idea is to study the bias among different agents found in the images such as, in the case of people, their gender, whether they are children or adults, etc. and in the case of vehicles, their color, whether it is a bicycle, a segway or others.


How does it work?


Images will be displayed automatically. All images will contain one or several bounding boxes highlighting an agent in the image.

  • Click inside the bounding box to select the agent and a list of possible tags will appear on the right.
  • Select as many tags as you consider fit the description of the agent (keep in mind tags must be consistent e.g. a man/woman person does not exist).
  • Once you are finished click the "Next image" button to step forward to the next image.
